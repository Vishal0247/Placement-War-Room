# Placement War Room (PWR) - Hackathon Project

## 🚨 The Problem Statement
Every year, millions of students graduate with degrees in technology, yet a significant percentage remain unemployable due to a massive gap between academic curricula and industry expectations. Students face three critical bottlenecks when preparing for placements:
1. **Lack of Personalized Guidance:** Generic roadmaps (e.g., "Learn DSA, then Web Dev") fail to account for a student's current skill level, their specific target company (e.g., Google vs. a local startup), or their target role.
2. **The "Blind Spot" in Skill Gaps:** Students often do not realize their resume is incompatible with ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) or that they are missing crucial niche skills until they face consecutive rejections.
3. **Information Overload & Burnout:** The sheer volume of resources available online leads to decision paralysis. Students need actionable, day-by-step missions rather than overwhelming documentation.

## 💡 Our Solution
**Placement War Room (PWR)** is an AI-powered command center designed to act as a hyper-personalized, dynamic mentor for students. By leveraging Gemini AI, PWR transforms a student's raw profile and resume into a targeted, actionable battle plan to conquer their dream company's interviews.

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Dynamic AI Curriculum Engine
Instead of generic advice, PWR generates a highly structured, 10-level roadmap (Missions) customized exclusively for the user's target company and role. It bridges the exact gap between their current knowledge and the company's expectations, providing step-by-step actionable tasks.

### 2. Intelligent Skill Gap Matrix
PWR analyzes the student's uploaded CV against the specific job requirements of their target company. It definitively categorizes their technical stack into **Acquired Skills** and **Missing Requirements**, completely eliminating the guesswork from preparation.

### 3. AI Resume Analyzer (ATS)
The platform parses the user's PDF resume and runs it through an AI-driven ATS simulation. It returns a definitive compatibility score alongside actionable bullet-point feedback to optimize the resume for actual recruiters.

### 4. True Readiness Score
Using deep analysis, the AI evaluates the student's entire profile (Resume + Skills + Target Company) to calculate a brutal, honest "Readiness Percentage." This tells the student exactly how prepared they are to pass an interview *today*, along with a customized justification.

### 5. Persistent Mission Tracking
A gamified dashboard where students can track their progress, mark missions as completed, and manually add custom missions, keeping them motivated and on track.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
- **Frontend:** React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion (for fluid, premium animations)
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **AI Integration:** Google Gemini 2.0 Flash (Unified Master Onboarding Agent for optimized API performance)
- **Database:** Drizzle ORM for robust data persistence
- **File Processing:** `pdf-parse` for seamless resume extraction

## 🏆 Hackathon Impact & Viability
Placement War Room isn't just a basic LLM chat wrapper; it is a specialized orchestration of AI workflows designed to solve a critical, real-world problem for the student demographic. By optimizing API calls (reducing quota usage by 70%) and providing an incredibly premium UI, it is built to scale and provides an immediate, tangible improvement to a student's career trajectory.
